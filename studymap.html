StudyMap - Study Planner (Coming Soon)

This app is designed to help you organize your study life effortlessly, manage your tasks and classes, and stay on top of your academic goals with ease.

Features

  • Unlimited Tasks, Subjects, and Classes: Add as many tasks, subjects, and classes as you need to keep track of your study schedule without limits.
  • Calendar View: View your tasks and classes in a structured calendar format. Includes a daily overview for detailed planning and a monthly overview for a broader perspective.
  • Home Screen Overview: A home screen that displays your upcoming tasks and classes in a simple and actionable To-Do list.
  • Mark Completion: Easily mark tasks and classes as completed, helping you track your progress and stay motivated.
  • Offline Capabilities: Access your planner anytime, even without an internet connection. Your changes will sync automatically when you're back online.
  • Sync Across Multiple Devices: Seamlessly access your study planner on any device, ensuring you always have your schedule at your fingertips.
