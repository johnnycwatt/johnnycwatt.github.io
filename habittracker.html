Habit Tracker - Plan, Succeed

This app is designed to help you build and maintain habits effectively while tracking your progress with detailed statistics and visualizations.

Features

  • Flexible Habit Management:
    • Add, edit, and delete habits tailored to your lifestyle.
    • Customizable frequencies: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Custom (e.g., Mon-Wed-Fri or Sat-Sun).
    • Mark previous days as complete via the Progress Screen.
  • Progress Tracking:
    • Current Streaks and Best Streaks.
    • Detailed Weekly and Monthly Completion Rates.
    • Weekly and Monthly Consistency Metrics.
    • Completion Calendar highlighting completed dates in light green.
  • Reminders: Local notifications for habits due on their due dates.
  • Light and Dark Mode: Seamlessly switch between light and dark themes.
  • Reports and Insights:
    • Comprehensive Monthly Reports with color-coded completion rates.
    • Summary section featuring best-performing habit, total habits tracked, average completion rate, and frequently missed habits.
  • Data Ownership: Export habit progress and data to CSV files for backup or further analysis.
